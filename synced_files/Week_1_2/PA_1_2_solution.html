Assume that the length and width each have a Normal distribution as follows: $$ height \sim \mathcal{N}(5000, 25) \\ width \sim \mathcal{N}(2000, 100) $$

  4. Compute the mean and standard deviation of area
  5. Compute the PDF of area and add it to the histogram
